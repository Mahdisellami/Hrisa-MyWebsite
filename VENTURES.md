## Action Items

For each venture, we need to define:

1. **Elevator Pitch** (1-2 sentences)
   - What is it?
   - Who is it for?
   - What problem does it solve?

2. **Problem Statement** (2-3 sentences)
   - Why does this matter?
   - Who experiences this problem?
   - Current inadequate solutions?

3. **Solution Overview** (3-4 sentences)
   - How does your approach differ?
   - Key features/capabilities
   - Why now? (timing, technology enablers)

4. **Status & Next Steps**
   - Current stage
   - What you're working on
   - What you need (help, feedback, etc.)

5. **Visual Assets**
   - Concept diagram or mockup
   - Logo/icon
   - Screenshots (if applicable)

- "Innovation Lab" or "Ideas I'm Exploring"
- Transparent about stage (concept vs MVP vs launched)
- Invitation to collaborate rather than sales pitch


- Show that you're the right person to build this

  - "Hrisa Code draws on my full-stack expertise across multiple modern frameworks"
